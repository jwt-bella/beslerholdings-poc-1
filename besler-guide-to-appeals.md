# BESLER Webinar: Guide to Appeals

**Date:** April 3, 2024  
**Source:** https://www.besler.com/insights/guide-to-appeals/

## Overview

Industry experts offer a diverse perspective on the appeal process and share valuable insights to benefit healthcare organizations.

**Speakers:**
- Christina Brown – Director of Reimbursement, BESLER
- Kristin DeGroat – General Counsel, BESLER
- Leslie Goldsmith – Partner at Bass, Berry & Sim
