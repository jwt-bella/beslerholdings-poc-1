About Eric

Eric Englebretson serves as Vice President of Information Technology at Besler Holdings, where he leads the organization's IT infrastructure, network operations, and systems administration.

IT Leadership

As VP of IT, Eric oversees Besler's technology infrastructure ensuring reliable, secure, and efficient systems that support the company's operations and client services. He manages critical IT systems including network infrastructure, servers, and enterprise applications.

Technical Proficiency

Eric possesses deep expertise in network and systems administration. He is highly proficient with routers, switches, firewalls, and wireless equipment from Cisco and other leading vendors. His technical skills extend to managing Active Directory, Exchange, Windows networking services, and other critical infrastructure for both LAN and Internet operations.

Hardware & Systems Management

He excels at selecting, installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting hardware systems across the organization. His methodical approach ensures maximum uptime and optimal performance for all IT assets.
