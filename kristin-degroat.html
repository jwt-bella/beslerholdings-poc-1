About Kristin

Kristin DeGroat serves as Chief Legal Officer of Besler Holdings, where she oversees all legal and compliance-related functions. She provides strategic legal counsel and ensures the company's operations meet the highest standards of regulatory compliance.

Legal & Compliance Leadership

As Chief Legal Officer, Kristin directs Besler's legal strategy and compliance programs. Her expertise spans healthcare reimbursement law, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance—critical areas for a company serving healthcare providers nationwide.

Professional Experience

Before joining Besler in 2018, Kristin held consulting roles at many leading healthcare consulting organizations. Her work focused on Cost Report Reimbursement, where she provided legal counsel and compliance support to healthcare clients navigating complex reimbursement regulations.

Thought Leadership

Kristin is a recognized thought leader in healthcare compliance and reimbursement law. She has authored articles, participated in podcasts, and hosted webinars sharing her expertise with the healthcare community.
